About

IMAGE COMING SOON...

My name is Terrance Blakely, I am a web developer and fine artist. I produce web applications and websites for individuals pursuing creative endeavors. I come largely from a creative background involving graphic design and fine art drawing and painting. So I when developing websites and applications I give much focus to aesthetically pleasing designs and engaging presentations.

I spent a lot of time pursuing academic degrees mainly from the arts sector but in the end I never finished it all out. Not the funnest thing to admit, but what school offers ultimately is what I feel I stepped away from everything and learned. That is the understanding of having a process, having a idea, starting creating something, and finishing it to a point ready to be published. When I'm not doing development and things of the fine art variety, I am often getting involved in a sport such as basketball. I also am a fan of dogs, and reading technical books.
